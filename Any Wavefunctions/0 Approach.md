If all information required to determine the kinetic energy $T$ were contained in the density $n$, then all sets of wavefunctions $\{\phi_i\}$ that reproduce the same density $n$, would necessarily yield the same kinetic energy $T$.

It would then follow that, for a given density $n$, the kinetic energy $T$ could be obtained by constructing any single one of the infinitely many wavefunction sets $\{\phi_i\}$ reproducing $n$, without regard to the specific choice of representation.
